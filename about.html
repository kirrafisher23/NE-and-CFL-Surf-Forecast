About

I’m Kirra, raised in Satellite Beach currently based in Jacksonville. Surfing has been an integral part of my life as I grew up living so close to the water. I developed a deep passion for understanding how the ocean works, especially the science behind surf forecasting. From wind directions to swell periods and angles.

Over the years, I’ve had the chance to travel and surf in places like New Zealand and Nicaragua. It’s been an incredible privilege to compare how different regions work with their own unique swell systems and wind patterns. Each spot has its own rhythm, and learning how to read the local conditions has only deepened my love and interest for forecasting.

I earned my degree in computer science from UNF, and combining my passion for surfing with computing this website felt like the perfect tinker project. This website is inspired by my dad’s knowledge of the local surf scene along Florida’s east coast, and all the old websites he used while I was growing up. More ideas are coming soon!
